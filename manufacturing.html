Manufacturing

Nexgrid has the highest priority to ensure our products are manufactured to the highest standard. This means that not only does the factory operate to internationally recognized standards of production and quality control but every single product is tested at the end of production to ensure performance.


Nexgrid products are manufactured under strict controls


  • ISO 9001:2000
  • ISO 14001:1996
  • TS 16949:2002
  • RoHS compliance
  • Active Environmental policy

SMT facility

Large capacity SMT lines ensure volume production targets can be achieved. Nexgrid can provide its customers requiring a handful of modules through to millions of modules per year for large scale deployment of smart grid products.


Total of 10 SMT production lines with total daily output: 11,200k chips


Automatic optical inspection ensures excellent QA on small modules


Automatic X-Ray inspection on critical surface mounted or shielded components


4 super high-speed Philips lines and 2 Siemens lines


Juki SMT equipment


Lead-free Process operational


Capable of handling 0201, flexible PCB, and BGA components


Full serial number and labeling barcode scanning system on the production line and warehouse ensures full traceability from components to finished goods
